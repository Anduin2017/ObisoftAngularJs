What's the difference between teams and infostations? Don't know what to create? Check first!

Team and Info Station are both Group. But the two type of groups have different function and facing different kind of users.

Team is small group of people working together on some projects, like a small corporation. Team can focus on several projects at the same time. Different projects have independent events and tasks.

People in a Info Station do NOT work together, but have the same tasks and events. Like a math class, or a travel group. All people share the same events and tasks in one Info Station.
